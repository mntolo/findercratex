Looking for quality assets for your project? Search no more!

Three sets of illustations from three top-notch polish illustrators. For free, unlimited business use.

At UIG, working with startups is our everyday life. After all, it is thanks to them that new opportunities and projects constantly appear. That’s why we decided to think about how we can help startups that have not yet achieved great business success but are growing in strength.
